// Ecosystem Treasury Cycle
1
Treasuries Funded
CashX, DistroX, and DIVX each build treasury depth through launch capital, taxes, and ongoing ecosystem activity.
2
Capital Deployed into DeFi
Treasury funds move into yield-generating PulseChain positions and strategic liquidity routes.
3
Yield Harvested and Rotated
A portion compounds the treasury base while another portion supports buy-burns, rewards, and farm incentives.
4
Buy Pressure Across the Ecosystem
DistroX supports CashX and DistroX burns. DIVX supports its own treasury cycle, farms, reflections, and burns.
5
Supply Shrinks, Rewards Flow
Burned tokens are removed permanently while farms and reflections keep active holders connected to the whole ecosystem.
