// Treasury Cycle
1
SAC Closes → Treasury Funded
All sacrifice proceeds move to the treasury. Capital is ready to deploy immediately at launch.
2
Capital Deployed into DeFi
Funds enter yield-generating positions across PulseChain — Emit, Actuator, Tru2Farms and more.
3
Yield Harvested
Generated yield is harvested. A portion compounds the treasury base. The rest goes to buy-and-burn.
4
Treasury Buys $CASHX
Harvested yield is used to buy $CASHX directly on the open market — creating real buy pressure.
5
Tokens Burned Permanently
Purchased tokens sent to the burn address. Circulating supply decreases. Return to step 3 — indefinitely.
🏦
Active From Day One
SAC proceeds deploy immediately into yield-generating DeFi positions. No vesting, no waiting — the treasury starts working the moment it's funded.
📊
Stablecoin Strategy at Highs
When PulseChain runs, treasury positions rotate to stablecoins to lock in gains. Capital is redeployed at strategic entries — compounding the base before the next burn cycle.
🔥
Manual Buy & Burn
Burns are executed manually by the team. Revenue from yield is used to buy $CASHX on the open market, creating real buy pressure, then burned permanently.
